WHAT WE DO

SECURITY GRADES

A security grade is a classification system used to evaluate the level of protection provided by security systems, personnel, or devices. It helps determine the effectiveness and reliability of security measures based on predefined standards.

CASH IN TRANSIT

Cash-in-Transit (CIT) security refers to the safeguarding of cash and other valuable items during transportation between locations, such as banks, retail outlets, or ATMs. This specialized security service is designed to prevent theft, loss, or damage during transit.

REACTION

Reaction security refers to a specialized security service that responds promptly to alarms, threats, or emergencies at a specific location. The goal is to provide rapid intervention to mitigate risks, protect assets, and ensure safety.
